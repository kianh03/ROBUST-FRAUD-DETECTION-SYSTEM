Our Mission

At FraudTest, we are dedicated to making the internet a safer place. Our mission is to provide cutting-edge fraud detection solutions that protect users and businesses from online threats, phishing attempts, and malicious websites.

We believe that access to reliable security tools shouldn't be complicated or expensive. That's why we've developed an easy-to-use platform powered by advanced artificial intelligence to give everyone the ability to verify the safety of URLs before visiting them.

How We Calculate Risk

Risk Calculation Methodology

Our fraud detection system calculates risk based on three main components:

  • URL Features (40%): Analysis of URL characteristics like length, special characters, and domain properties.
  • Suspicious Patterns (50%): Detection of common phishing and fraud patterns.
  • Domain Information (10%): Analysis of domain age, popularity, and hosting details.

URL Features We Analyze

  • Domain length and entropy
  • Special character frequency
  • TLD (Top-Level Domain) risk score
  • Presence of suspicious elements (e.g., @, IP addresses, encoded characters)
  • URL length and structure
  • Suspicious domain naming patterns
  • Protocol security (HTTP vs HTTPS)

Suspicious Patterns Detection

  • Phishing indicators (e.g., login, password, verify in URLs)
  • Brand impersonation attempts
  • Redirect chains and cloaking techniques
  • Character substitution (e.g., using "0" instead of "o")
  • Abnormal subdomain structures
  • Keyword stuffing patterns
  • Deceptive URL shorteners

Domain Analysis

  • Domain age and registration details
  • Hosting location and provider reputation
  • SSL certificate validation
  • Traffic patterns and popularity signals
  • WHOIS data analysis
  • IP reputation assessment
  • Geographic risk analysis
