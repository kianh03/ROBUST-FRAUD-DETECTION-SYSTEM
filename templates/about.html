How We Calculate Risk

Our fraud detection system uses a sophisticated multi-factor approach to analyze URLs and determine their risk level. We combine machine learning with heuristic analysis to provide accurate risk assessments.

Risk Calculation Methodology

Our fraud detection system calculates risk based on three main components:

  • URL Features (60%): Analysis of URL characteristics like length, special characters, and domain properties.
  • Suspicious Patterns (30%): Detection of common phishing and fraud patterns.
  • Domain Information (10%): Analysis of domain age, popularity, and hosting details.

HTTPS security reduces the risk by approximately 20%, as secure connections are typically associated with legitimate websites.

URL Features We Analyze

  • Domain length and entropy
  • Special character frequency
  • TLD (Top-Level Domain) risk score
  • Presence of suspicious elements (e.g., @, IP addresses, encoded characters)
  • URL length and structure

Suspicious Patterns Detection

  • Phishing indicators (e.g., login, password, verify in URLs)
  • Brand impersonation attempts
  • Redirect chains and cloaking techniques
  • Character substitution (e.g., using "0" instead of "o")
  • Abnormal subdomain structures

Domain Analysis

  • Domain age and registration details
  • Hosting location and provider reputation
  • SSL certificate validation
  • Traffic patterns and popularity signals
