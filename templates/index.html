How We Calculate Risk

The fraud risk score is based on a comprehensive analysis that considers:

  • URL features (60%): Analysis of domain, path, parameters, and other URL components
  • Suspicious patterns (30%): Identification of known phishing or scam patterns
  • Domain information (10%): Background information about the domain and its hosting

HTTPS security decreases risk by 20% for trusted domains.
